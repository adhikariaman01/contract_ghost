RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Residential Lease Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 1, 2024, between Landlord Corp LLC ("Landlord") and Tenant Name ("Tenant").

PROPERTY: 123 Main Street, Los Angeles, California 90001

1. TERM
The lease term begins January 1, 2024, and ends December 31, 2024. This agreement shall automatically renew for successive one-year terms unless either party provides 30-day written notice of intent not to renew.

2. RENT AND PAYMENT
Tenant agrees to pay $2,500 per month, due on the 1st of each month. A late fee of $200 shall be assessed for payments received after the 5th. Tenant waives all rights to interest on the security deposit.

3. HABITABILITY WAIVER
Tenant accepts the property in its current "as-is" condition and expressly waives any and all claims related to habitability, including the right to repair and deduct. Tenant acknowledges that Landlord has no obligation to maintain the property in a habitable condition during the term of this lease.

4. LIABILITY LIMITATION
Landlord shall not be liable for any injury, damage, or loss to Tenant or Tenant's property, including injury or loss caused by Landlord's gross negligence or intentional misconduct. Tenant releases Landlord from all liability whatsoever.

5. ARBITRATION
All disputes arising from this Agreement shall be resolved exclusively by binding arbitration. Tenant waives the right to a jury trial and to participation in any class action lawsuit. Arbitration shall be conducted under rules selected solely by Landlord.

6. NON-COMPETE / EXCLUSIVITY
Tenant agrees not to reside in any other residential property within 50 miles of the leased premises for a period of two years following termination of this lease.

7. ENTRY AND INSPECTION
Landlord reserves the right to enter the premises at any time, without notice, for any reason, including inspection and showing to prospective tenants or buyers.

8. SUBLETTING
Tenant shall not sublet or assign this Agreement without prior written consent of Landlord. Landlord may withhold consent for any reason or no reason at all.

9. SECURITY DEPOSIT
A security deposit of $5,000 is required. Landlord may use the security deposit for any purpose at Landlord's sole discretion, including general business expenses, and is not required to return any portion of the deposit.

10. PETS
No pets of any kind are permitted. A non-refundable pet fee of $1,000 applies if any pet is found on the premises, regardless of whether Tenant owns the pet.

11. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.

12. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. No verbal representations or promises are binding.
